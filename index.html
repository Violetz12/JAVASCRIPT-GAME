Escape the cat!

We've created an interactive experience with lovely P5.JS and we're excited to have you check it out!!! Its an escape room game where you solve puzzles to escape the cat. The game is designed to be played on desktop or laptop devices. If you encounter issues of game not displaying, please try resizing your browser window!

How to play
